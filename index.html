Trading Pattern Reference Library
Double Bottom Reversal

Structure: Price drops twice to similar support levels, separated by a peak. The pattern is completed when price breaks above the peak ("neckline").

AI Strategy: Set buy entries just above neckline breakouts. Place stop losses below the lowest swing bottom. Set targets equal to neckline height.

Head and Shoulders Reversal

Structure: Forms three peaks: a higher peak (head) in between two lower, roughly equal peaks (shoulders). Neckline connects the intermediate troughs.

AI Strategy: Enter short trades upon a clean breakdown below the neckline. Place stops above the right shoulder peak. Take profit targets equal head height measured downwards.

Bull Flag Continuation

Structure: Strong upward pole move, followed by a slight downward-sloping consolidation channel (the flag). Breakout above channel signals continuation.

AI Strategy: Enter at the breakout point of upper flag border. Place stop loss beneath the lowest flag border. Measure pole size to calculate target levels.

Cup and Handle Continuation

Structure: A rounded bowl-shaped accumulation basin followed by a brief downward flag (handle). Completion is marked by breaking above bowl lip resistance.

AI Strategy: Enter buy/long setups on breakout from handle resistance. Stop loss levels placed mid-way down the handle. Target height is depth of the cup.
